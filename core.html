static evaluate_shortcut(func)[source]¶

Annotation that applies the “evaluator guard” to an evaluator.

A child class may apply this annotation to evaluate. Doing so

prevents the evaluator from re-scoring genomes that already have a fitness.

This may accelerate learning, as individuals retained from the

parent generation are no longer re-evaluated. However, doing so also prevents the evaluator from correctly modeling a changing fitness landscape, where the fitness of an individual may change across generations.
